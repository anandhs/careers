## How to use this site

Use **categories** when you already know the broad field, such as Technology, Healthcare, or Engineering.

Use **tags** when you want to compare jobs across fields, such as `remote-friendly`, `licensure-required`, `field-work`, or `high-growth`.

Each page follows the same structure so it is easy to compare roles side by side.
