title: "About"
description: "Why this site exists and how to use it."
---

Career Atlas is a simple career-exploration site for people who want practical, readable career pages.

Each career page is designed to answer a few questions quickly:

- What is this job really?
- What does a normal day look like?
- What does it pay?
- How do people get into it?
- What are the tradeoffs?
- What might change over the next 10 years?

The goal is not to predict the future perfectly. The goal is to help readers compare paths more clearly.
