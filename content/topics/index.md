---
title: "How To Use Tags"
description: "How categories and tags organize the site."
---

This site uses two simple organizing tools:

- `category` for the major career family, such as Technology or Healthcare
- `tags` for cross-cutting traits, such as `remote-friendly`, `licensure-required`, `field-work`, or `high-growth`

That means one career can be easy to discover in more than one way without moving files into nested folders.
